Our Mission

At Drexel Foot Traffic, our mission is to enable you to make the most out of your day. We want you to have the power at your fingertips to know what's busy around campus and enable you to better plan your day

Why Choose Us?

  • Real Time Updates: We scrape from Google's API using an API that we built, so all data about business is live!
  • Built by Drexel Students for Drexel Students We know what it's like to traverse across campus just to be met with a massive line. We were sick of it too.
  • Convenience:Every building you'll ever need information on, we have!
